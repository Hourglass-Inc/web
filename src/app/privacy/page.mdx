# Privacy Policy

_Effective Date: July 30, 2025_

At **Tymli**, your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines the data we collect, how we use it, and the principles we follow to ensure your information is handled responsibly.

---

## 1. What We Collect

When you use Tymli, we collect the following information to personalize your experience and improve our services:

### Account Information

- **Email address** (from Google Sign-In)  
- **Google avatar** (profile image)  
- **User token** (internally generated for secure session management)  

### User Preferences and Goals

Collected during onboarding or profile setup:

- Name  
- Job role  
- Team mode (e.g., solo, paired, or group workflows)  
- Work type (e.g., remote, hybrid, office)  
- Daily work hours  
- Distraction apps you wish to block  
- Distraction content types (e.g., videos, social media)  
- Estimated distraction time  
- Productivity goals  
- Enforcement preferences (e.g., how strictly distractions are blocked)  
- Referral source (how you found Tymli)  

---

## 2. Why We Collect This Information

We use your data to:

- Personalize your productivity dashboard  
- Enable distraction-blocking features  
- Track and visualize your focus patterns  
- Offer recommendations and accountability tools  
- Improve Tymli based on aggregate user behavior  

---

## 3. How Your Data is Stored and Protected

- All user data is securely stored and encrypted.  
- Internally generated tokens are used to maintain secure sessions.  
- Access to your data is restricted to authorized systems and team members only.  

---

## 4. What We Don’t Do

- We **do not** sell your data to third parties.  
- We **do not** use your data to train AI models — at least not right now.  
  If that ever changes, we’ll update this policy transparently and ask for your permission.  

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

We use Google for authentication. Google may collect certain information according to its  
[Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## 6. Changes to This Policy

As Tymli evolves, this Privacy Policy may be updated.  
Major changes will be communicated via email or in-app notifications.

---

## 7. Contact Us

For questions, suggestions, or to request data deletion, please contact:  
**support@tymli.org**
